[Scout] I was fortunate to be sent to Mrs. Lucretia. From what I knew of her, she was kinder than her brother. However, the relief was short-lived. Slavery still bound us, and the fear of Master Andrew remained, always lurking in the shadows. But I clung to hope, knowing that even in the darkest times, there's a glimmer of light somewhere.
